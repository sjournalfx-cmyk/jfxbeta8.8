## Replies to expect (and how to handle them)

When these posts go out, people will reply. Some replies you'll get and how to respond:

- **"what broker do you use?"** → "depends on the trader. i won't recommend one in DM but the journal works with any MT5 broker — just plug the desktop bridge in."
- **"is this free?"** → "yes. free for 10-50 beta testers. DM me if you want in."
- **"is this for crypto/stocks too?"** → "built for MT5 forex. crypto/stocks would be a different build. maybe later."
- **"how is this different from [competitor]?"** → "most journals log the trade. mine logs the state behind the trade. that's the gap."
- **"this is a scam"** → don't reply. mute. move on.
- **"i blew my account, would this help?"** → "yes. that's literally why i built it. DM me."
