# JournalFX — Social & Community Content

This folder contains 2 weeks of ready-to-post content for the JournalFX beta launch, the Twitter engagement playbook, and the Facebook (Page + Groups) strategy for the SA market.

## Read this first

**Order of operations:**

1. **Rebrand your Twitter** — update bio, profile pic, header, then post + pin the pinned tweet. See `bio-and-pin.md`.
2. **Open the engagement playbook** — `engagement-playbook.md`. Add 30 forex accounts to a Twitter List, spend 30 min/day replying with the 5 templates.
3. **Post the calendar** — `content-calendar.md` shows you what to post when. The actual post copy is in `twitter-week-1.md` and `twitter-week-2.md`.
4. **Mirror to Telegram** — `telegram-week-1-2.md` has 4 ready-to-send posts. Send on the days marked in the calendar.
5. **Set up Facebook** — `facebook-week-1-2.md` has 4 Page posts to mirror + a full strategy for SA Facebook Groups (this is the highest-leverage community channel for you).

## Channel priorities

| Priority | Channel | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Twitter** | Primary outbound + engagement. Repurposes to everything else. |
| 2 | **Facebook Groups (SA)** | The *real* community channel for SA forex traders. Where you get discovered. |
| 3 | **Facebook Page** | Broadcast mirror. Slightly more polished than Twitter, longer form. |
| 4 | **Telegram** | Broadcast mirror. No real community yet (0 followers) — that's OK for now. |

## Time commitment

- ~30 min/day, 5-7 days a week for Twitter engagement
- ~15 min/day, 3 days a week for posting on Twitter
- ~15 min, 4 days a week for Facebook Page posts + group comments
- ~15 min, 1 day a week for Telegram mirrors
- **Total: ~2-2.5 hours/week**

## Voice

Casual founder. Lowercase on Twitter. Sentence case on Facebook (slightly more polished, still casual). First person. Short sentences. No hashtags (or 1-2 max). Reply to every comment on your posts within 24 hours (Twitter) and 1 hour (Facebook — algorithm rewards early engagement).

## What this is NOT

- This is not a polished corporate content calendar. It reads like a real founder talking, because that's what performs in the trading niche.
- This is not a 30-day plan. After week 2, review what worked, kill what didn't, and we'll draft weeks 3-4 informed by real data.
- This is not a "post and forget" system. The daily engagement routine is the real growth driver. The posts are the bait; the replies are the rod.
